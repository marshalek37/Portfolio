About Me

Hello there! I'm Patryk, front - end developer passionate about building fun and interactive web experiences. My main goal is creating useful websites that offer a smooth user experience.

My interest in technology led me to pursue front - end development career. Using HTML, CSS / SCSS, JavaScript / TypeScript, REACT and other utilities to bring design thoughts to life, I appreciate the process of turning them into clean, effective code.

I've always found inspiration in the rhythm and melody of music, which has always played a major role in my life. Music inspires me and feeds my creativity, whether it's through producing or listening to music.

I enjoy traveling a lot, and I am always looking for new experiences, different perspectives, and ways of thinking that can inspire my work. It's incredible how many different factors can influence on how we approach our design.

Cars are also big fascination of mine. From their sleek design to their powerful engines, I admire the engineering craft that goes into them.

Last but not least, I love cats with. Their humorous nature and independent spirit make my life more joyful. They serve as a constant reminder to me to approach problems with curiosity and versatility.

I'm excited about the chance to work alongside professionals on interesting tasks, continue to learn and progress. Let's collaborate and creating something great!
