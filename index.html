About Me

Hello there! My name is Patryk, and I am a dedicated front-end developer with a profound passion for crafting engaging and dynamic web experiences. I am driven by the desire to create websites that not only meet the needs of users but also provide them with a seamless and enjoyable browsing experience.

My journey into the world of front-end development was spurred by my keen interest in technology. I possess a comprehensive skill set that includes proficiency in a variety of tools and languages, such as HTML, CSS/SCSS, JavaScript/TypeScript, React, and other essential utilities. Through these, I have honed my ability to seamlessly translate design concepts into clean, efficient code that brings visual ideas to life.

I am thrilled at the prospect of collaborating with seasoned professionals on stimulating projects, as I am continuously seeking opportunities to expand my knowledge and expertise. My extensive proficiency in a wide range of technologies, including TypeScript, JavaScript (ES2015-2019), React, Redux, HTML5, CSS3, Sass (SCSS), BEM, LESS, and various others, combined with my familiarity with tools such as Figma, GitHub, VS Code, and Chrome Developer Tools, has prepared me to excel in dynamic and demanding work environments.

Furthermore, my experience with project management tools like ClickUp and Trello, coupled with my proficiency in Agile and Scrum methodologies, enables me to thrive in collaborative settings and contribute to the seamless execution of tasks. My familiarity with WordPress, Bootstrap, ESLint, Fetch, REST API, SQL, Node, Express, and NPM further solidifies my capability to deliver high-quality, robust, and user-friendly web solutions.

I am excited about the opportunity to join your esteemed team and contribute to the creation of exceptional digital experiences. Let's collaborate and build something remarkable together!
